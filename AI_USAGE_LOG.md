# AI Contract & Usage Log 
## AI Usage Acknowledgment
We, the undersigned members of the TechCare Team, hereby agree that while AI tools may assist in brainstorming, drafting documentation, and debugging code syntax, they will not replace our core understanding of the software engineering process. 

## Operational Policies
1.**Repository Logging:** Every instance of AI assistance regarding core application mechanics will be continuously documented within this file.
2. **Data Security:** No confidential client metrics, personal identifiers, or database schemas belonging to the Akuapem North Community Health Center will be shared with public AI models.
3. **Individual Accountability:** Every team member assumes full personal responsibility for explaining the internal logic of the source code they submit. If a contributor cannot articulate how their code works during evaluations, they forfeit all project credit.
